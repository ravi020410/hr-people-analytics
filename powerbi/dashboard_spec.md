# Power BI Dashboard Specification

## Page 1: Headcount and Attrition Overview

- KPI cards: active employees, voluntary exits, attrition rate, average tenure.
- Bar chart: headcount by department.
- Bar chart: attrition rate by department.
- Slicers: department, role, performance score, tenure bucket.

## Page 2: Attrition Factors and Compensation Drill-down

- Bar chart: attrition by pay competitiveness bucket.
- Bar chart: job satisfaction by pay competitiveness bucket.
- Matrix: department, role, salary deviation, attrition rate.
- Decision focus: identify underpaid high performers and first-year retention risks.
